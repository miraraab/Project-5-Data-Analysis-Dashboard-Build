# Opportunities, Risks & Hype Mapping

## Opportunities

### Primary Opportunity: Continuous Strategy Monitoring
| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| Business Value | Reduce reporting cycle from annual to monthly/quarterly |
| Time Saving | Est. 20-40% reduction in manual reporting effort |
| Decision Quality | Earlier course correction = fewer strategic derailments |
| Scalability | Once built, marginal cost of adding KPIs is low |

### Secondary Opportunities
- **Standardization:** AI layer forces KPI definitions to be consistent across departments
- **Transparency:** Leadership gets unified view instead of fragmented department reports
- **Accountability:** Deviations are flagged automatically, not buried in slide decks

---

## Risks

### Technical Risks
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Poor data quality in legacy systems | High | High | Start with 3-5 clean KPIs, expand later |
| No unified data model across departments | High | Medium | Pilot in one department first |
| Integration complexity with existing IT | Medium | High | Use lightweight API layer, not full integration |

### Organizational Risks
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Change resistance from department heads | High | High | Co-design process, not top-down rollout |
| KPI gaming once monitoring is automated | Medium | Medium | Include qualitative check-ins alongside data |
| Dependency on single vendor | Low | Medium | Open-source first approach |

### Compliance Risks
| Risk | Detail |
|---|---|
| GDPR | No personal employee data in monitoring layer — use aggregated metrics only |
| BSI (IT-Sicherheit) | Federal agencies require BSI-compliant cloud solutions — limits vendor options |
| Political sensitivity | Strategy deviations visible to more people — requires governance framework |

## Hype vs. Evidence Mapping

| Claim | Source Type | Verdict |
|---|---|---|
| "AI makes strategy execution 3x faster" | Vendor (McKinsey/BCG reports) | ⚠️ Overhyped — depends entirely on data readiness |
| "AI replaces strategic planning" | Media/Vendor | ❌ False — AI is a reporting layer, not a decision engine |
| "Public sector AI adoption is growing" | Gartner 2024 | ✅ Supported — 67% of gov orgs piloting AI by 2025 |
| "Strategy monitoring AI has clear ROI" | Deloitte Public Sector Report | ⚠️ Partially supported — ROI proven for reporting efficiency, not for decision quality |
| "Implementation takes 3-6 months" | Vendor claims | ⚠️ Optimistic — data readiness phase alone takes 2-4 months in large orgs |
