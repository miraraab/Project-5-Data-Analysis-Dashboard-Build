# Market Research: AI Adoption in Public Sector Strategy Monitoring

## Sector Overview
- **Sector:** Public Sector / Federal Agencies (Germany + EU context)
- **Focus:** Strategy execution monitoring, performance management, digital transformation

## Key Market Signals

### AI Adoption in Public Sector (2023–2025)
- Gartner (2024): 67% of government organizations are piloting or deploying AI — up from 34% in 2022
  *Source type: Industry analyst — treat as directional, not precise*
- EU AI Act (2024): Classifies most government AI applications as "high-risk" — requires additional compliance steps
  *Source type: Regulatory fact — verified*
- Digitalstrategie der Bundesregierung (2025): Explicit mandate for AI adoption in federal administration
  *Source type: Government document — verified*
- Deloitte Government Trends 2024: "Continuous performance monitoring" listed as top-3 AI use case for public sector
  *Source type: Consulting report — treat as indicative*
